Thanks for help us test our game. Some of the elements have been left out in order to keep some things secret. The graphics are not completed yet, so you are playing a prototype of the game. This game is viewed best full screen.

Here are the controls:

  • Movement --> WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Break light gray blocks --> Space key
  • Pull/move white blocks --> F Key & pushing them
  • Flip triangle switches --> E key

Link - Crystalara Game Link

We have left out the dialogue scene to keep things secret, click to adavance to the first level. Sometimes you might have to refresh the page in order to get back out of a place --> like the credits area.

This is a puzzle type game, that will require you to figure out what to do next. When in doubt...clear the light gray blocks, move the white blocks, and find/flip all the switches. One level has A LOT of switches!

Helps us by giving some constructive feedback about the game, keeping in mind that it is not complete yet. If you find a glitch, please describe it as best as you can.
